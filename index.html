What is 4DataPrivacy?

Our mission is to bring awareness to the public about data rights and privacy laws and advocate for their passing on local, state, and federal levels.

4DataPrivacy is a group of high school and college students studying data rights and privacy that was created through AI4All’s Special Interest Group initiative for alumni. AI4All is a US-based nonprofit dedicated to increasing diversity and inclusion in AI education, research, development, and policy; has 15 residential summer camps in partnerships with colleges across the US; and hosts 8 Special Interest Groups across various topics every year.

We aim to further our mission in three ways:

  • Create a website that breaks down information on data rights/privacy and includes the following two action items:
  • Letter-writing campaign for constituents to fill in our letter template and send it to their representatives. This letter advocates for the passing of the Right to Be Informed, the Right of Access, and the Right to Object/Contest.
  • Create a petition that will work to show support for the passing of the aforementioned data rights.

4DataPrivacy intends for these three items to be distributed for information awareness and sent to your representative to support the passing of the Right to be Informed, the Right of Access, and the Right to Object/Contest.

How You Can Contribute

  • Sign our petition

    We have created a petition that embodies some basic rights we can advocate for, and a template for you to email your representatives. The louder we are about this issue, the quicker change can happen.

  • Support organizations pushing for your data rights

    Data4Humanity and Encode Justice are student-led organizations focused on fighting for data rights and social justice in tech. STOP is an organization that litigates and legislation for data rights, and the Fight for the Future that promotes causes related to copyright legislation, as well as online privacy and censorship through the use of the Internet. Visit their websites, and donate and support them!

  • Educate yourself

    There are many ways to keep up to date with data rights and recent news stories. Sign up for the Propublica and Best of Privacy email list, and filter your favourite news app to give you information about Data and Privacy.

  • Be aware

    When you are signing terms of service, or working with companies that use data, make sure you understand what you are agreeing to.

  • Check before you buy

    Use Mozilla’s platform *Privacy Not Included to check for the privacy ranking of the products you want to buy.
